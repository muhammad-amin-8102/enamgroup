Our advanced Cow Mats from the 1300ZN Design Series, feature Diamond Amoeba top surfaces for exceptional comfort and grip. These mats are crafted with two side pattern options: a Regular Side Pattern and a 2 Side Interlock Pattern, ensuring versatile installation and enhanced stability.

Designed to provide optimal support and reduce fatigue, they elevate cattle off hard surfaces, promoting hygiene and reducing bacterial growth.

Ideal for dairy and livestock farms, these durable mats offer excellent insulation against cold floors, improving cattle welfare and productivity. Easy to clean and maintain, our cow mats are a long-term solution for livestock environments.

These are some features of our cow mat :

  • Comfortable support for cattle.
  • Promotes hygiene and cleanliness.
  • Insulates against cold floors.
  • Durable and long-lasting.
  • Enhances safety with improved traction.
  • Easy to clean and maintain.

Additional Care Instructions :

Regular Cleaning : Regularly sweep or vacuum mats to remove dirt, debris, and particles for cleanliness and longevity.

Spot Cleaning : Use mild soap and water promptly on spills or stains to maintain mat appearance and hygiene.

Avoid Harsh Chemicals : Protect mats by refraining from harsh chemicals that may degrade the rubber over time.

Drying : Ensure mats air dry completely before use to prevent moisture buildup and maintain durability.

Avoid Direct Sunlight : Shield mats from prolonged sunlight exposure to prevent fading and potential material degradation.

Storage : Store mats in a clean, dry area when not in use to extend their lifespan and prevent damage.

Rotation : Rotate mats regularly to distribute wear evenly, especially in high-traffic areas for prolonged durability.
