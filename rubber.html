About Us Delivering Best
Hydrowash Detergent Products

Enam Corporation of Companies stands as a leading manufacturer and exporter of premium Hydrowash detergent products, serving diverse industries across the globe. With a commitment to excellence that spans decades, we have established ourselves as a trusted partner for businesses seeking superior quality, effectiveness, and performance in cleaning solutions.

Our comprehensive product range includes specialized cleaning solutions for various applications, including multi-purpose detergents, commercial-grade formulations, eco-friendly options, and custom detergent products tailored to specific needs. Each product is meticulously formulated using advanced techniques and premium ingredients, ensuring unmatched quality that meets international standards.

With exports reaching over 18 countries worldwide, a dedicated team of 100+ skilled professionals, and a state-of-the-art manufacturing facility in Kolkata, India, we combine traditional expertise with modern innovation. Our unwavering focus on quality assurance, customer satisfaction, and sustainable practices has earned us the trust of clients globally, making us a preferred choice for Hydrowash detergent solutions that deliver lasting value.
